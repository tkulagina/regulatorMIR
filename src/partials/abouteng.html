About Us

  • The methodology for using MIR products was developed in close collaboration with the Kherson State Agrarian University.

  • More than 10 scientific articles have been published on the use of the MIR product, and a PhD thesis in agricultural sciences has been defended.

  • In 2017, the MIR product was included in the information collection "Innovative Developments of Universities and Scientific Institutions of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine."

  • Field trials have been conducted on private land plots and on research institute lands of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine since 2011.
