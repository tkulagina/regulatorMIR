Innovative Technologies for Agribusiness

The multipurpose immunoregulator of plant growth and development, MIR, enhances the effectiveness of plant protection products (PPP) and fertilizers by improving their absorption, distribution, and action. The MIR preparation allows for a 20-40% reduction in the use of PPP and fertilizers.

About Us

  • The methodology for using MIR products was developed in close collaboration with the Kherson State Agrarian University.

  • More than 10 scientific articles have been published on the use of the MIR product, and a PhD thesis in agricultural sciences has been defended.

  • In 2017, the MIR product was included in the information collection "Innovative Developments of Universities and Scientific Institutions of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine."

  • Field trials have been conducted on private land plots and on research institute lands of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine since 2011.

Advantages

  • Increases plant resistance to frost, moisture deficiency, second-harvest planting, diseases, etc.

  • Promotes the active development and deeper penetration of the root system.

  • Speeds up the growth of green mass and increases yield by 10-25%

  • Reduces nitrogen fertilizer consumption by 50% due to improved plant absorption.

  • Improves field germination of seeds and boosts plant growth energy.

  • Strengthens immunity and resistance to bacterial and fungal diseases.

  • Improves product quality (gluten content, proteins, starch, sugar, etc.).

  • Does not harm soil, the end product, or the environment.

  • MIR products are certified and listed in the Register of Pesticides and Agrochemicals Approved for Use in Ukraine.
